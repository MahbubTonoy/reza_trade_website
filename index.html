INTRODUCTION

Reza Trade International, established in Bangladesh in 1986, is a leading human resource company specializing in the recruitment of skilled professionals, semi-skilled workers, and laborers for international assignments in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Gulf countries, Malaysia, South Korea, and Singapore. Over the years, we have successfully recruited, trained, and deployed over 100,000 Bangladeshi workers across various countries through partnerships with 135 international recruiting agencies and companies.
